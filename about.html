About SkyReach

SkyReach provides a polished virtual airline experience with a focus on training, safe operations and community events. The site is intentionally lightweight and built to run entirely as static pages on GitHub Pages.

Mission

To give pilots a reliable, modern environment to learn, log and operate simulated flights while keeping data privacy in the pilot's control.

What we provide

  • Onboarding and Academy content for new pilots
  • Browser-native Pilot Hub with flight logging and fleet tools
  • Event coordination and community resources
